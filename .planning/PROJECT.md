# Curly Hair Collective

## Vision
A premium podcast & community platform celebrating curly hair culture — designed with a bold, Barbie-pink aesthetic that's unapologetically feminine and visually stunning.

## Target Audience
- Women with curly, coily, and wavy hair
- Natural hair enthusiasts and influencers
- Hair care professionals and stylists
- Anyone on their curly hair journey

## Brand Identity
- **Primary Color**: Barbie Pink (#E0218A / #FF69B4 / hot pink spectrum)
- **Secondary Colors**: Soft blush (#FFB6C1), cream white (#FFF5F5), deep magenta (#C71585)
- **Accent**: Gold (#FFD700) for premium touches
- **Typography**: Modern, bold sans-serif headers + elegant body text
- **Aesthetic**: Barbie-maximalist meets clean modern — pink gradients, playful curves, glitter accents
- **Mood**: Empowering, celebratory, luxe, community-driven

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Hero Section** — Bold podcast intro with host image, pink gradient background, listen-now CTAs
2. **Latest Episodes** — Card grid with episode artwork, guest photos, play buttons
3. **About Section** — Host bio with personal story + mission statement
4. **Community Section** — Newsletter signup, social links, community highlights
5. **Footer** — Streaming platform links (Spotify, Apple, YouTube), social media, legal

## Design Inspiration
- **Barbie.com** — Maximalist pink, glitter, femininity
- **Kylie Cosmetics** — Pale pink elegance, plush feminine vibes
- **Emma Gannon podcast** — Host-centric, colorful, personality-driven
- **CurlyWorld.com** — Clean podcast layout with episode cards, community engagement
- **Song Exploder** — Social media-inspired visual hierarchy
- **The Friday Habit** — Bold branding, bright CTA-driven design

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS v4
- Vercel deployment
- Responsive / mobile-first
