# Curly Hair Collective — Email Nurture & Sponsor Strategy

## Overview

Email nurture flows designed to convert subscribers into engaged community members, merch buyers, and sponsor-visible audience segments. Each flow is triggered by a specific action and delivers value-first content that naturally leads to conversions.

---

## Flow 1: Welcome Series (New Subscribers)

**Trigger**: Newsletter signup on any page
**Goal**: Build trust, establish value, drive first engagement
**Duration**: 7 days (5 emails)

| Day | Subject Line | Content | CTA |
|-----|-------------|---------|-----|
| 0 | Welcome to the Collective! | Brand intro, what to expect, host story | Listen to EP 01 |
| 1 | Your free curl type guide | Downloadable PDF: identify your curl pattern (2A-4C) | Download Guide |
| 3 | Our top 5 episodes for beginners | Curated list with descriptions | Browse Episodes |
| 5 | Wash day secrets from our expert guests | Best tips compiled from episodes | Share with a friend |
| 7 | You're officially in the Collective | Community social links, merch teaser | Follow on Instagram |

**Sponsor Integration**: Day 3 email can include a "Recommended by our partners" section with sponsor product placement.

---

## Flow 2: Episode Listener Nurture

**Trigger**: Clicks a specific episode link from email
**Goal**: Deepen engagement, cross-promote content
**Duration**: 5 days (3 emails)

| Day | Subject Line | Content | CTA |
|-----|-------------|---------|-----|
| 1 | Loved that episode? Here's the deep dive | Blog article related to the episode topic | Read Full Article |
| 3 | Products mentioned in this episode | Curated product list (affiliate/sponsor links) | Shop the Look |
| 5 | Related episodes you might love | 3 similar episodes based on topic/guest | Listen Now |

**Sponsor Integration**: Day 3 is the natural product placement email — sponsor products featured alongside organic recommendations.

---

## Flow 3: Merch Waitlist Nurture

**Trigger**: Joins merch waitlist (email on /merch page)
**Goal**: Build anticipation, drive launch-day sales
**Duration**: Pre-launch (sent in bursts before merch drop)

| Email | Subject Line | Content | CTA |
|-------|-------------|---------|-----|
| Confirmation | You're on the list! | Thank you, what's coming, sneak peeks | Share with friends |
| T-7 days | First look: our debut collection | Product previews, materials, sizing | Save your size |
| T-3 days | Early access starts in 3 days | Countdown, exclusive discount code | Set a reminder |
| T-1 day | Tomorrow. Your curls deserve this. | Final teaser, social proof from beta testers | Get ready |
| Launch | IT'S HERE. Shop the Collective. | Full collection reveal, early-bird pricing | Shop Now |
| T+3 days | Selling fast: your favorites are going | Best-sellers update, urgency | Shop Before Gone |

**Sponsor Integration**: Co-branded merch collaborations get dedicated emails in this flow.

---

## Flow 4: Re-engagement (Inactive Subscribers)

**Trigger**: No opens/clicks in 60 days
**Goal**: Win back or clean list (improves deliverability for sponsors)
**Duration**: 14 days (3 emails)

| Day | Subject Line | Content | CTA |
|-----|-------------|---------|-----|
| 0 | We miss you, queen! | Recap of what they've missed, best content | Come Back |
| 7 | Your curl type changed (probably) | Playful hook — seasonal care update | Read the Guide |
| 14 | Should we let you go? | Last chance to stay — straightforward ask | Stay / Unsubscribe |

Non-responders are moved to a suppressed segment (not deleted — available for re-activation campaigns).

---

## Flow 5: Sponsor Spotlight Series

**Trigger**: Manually scheduled (monthly or per sponsor campaign)
**Goal**: Drive sponsor ROI while maintaining audience trust
**Duration**: 1-3 emails per campaign

### Format A: Product Review Email
- Personal host review of sponsor product
- Before/after or routine integration
- Honest pros and cons
- Exclusive discount code for subscribers
- CTA: Shop with code

### Format B: Expert Q&A Email
- Sponsor expert answers community questions
- Educational value first, product mention second
- "Ask the Expert" community engagement
- CTA: Submit your question + shop

### Format C: Community Picks Email
- "What the Collective is loving this month"
- Mix of organic + sponsored products
- User testimonials and reviews
- CTA: Shop community favorites

## Segmentation Strategy

### Segments for Sponsor Targeting

| Segment | Criteria | Value to Sponsors |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| **Hot Leads** | Opened 3+ emails in 30 days | Highest engagement, best conversion |
| **Merch Buyers** | Previous purchase | Proven buyers, higher AOV |
| **New Subscribers** | Joined <30 days ago | Fresh audience, discovery phase |
| **Content Engagers** | Click-through on articles/episodes | Content-oriented, influence-able |
| **Curl Type Groups** | Self-identified curl pattern | Targeted product recommendations |
| **Parents** | Engaged with kids content | Family product opportunities |

### Data Points Collected
- Email address (required)
- First name (optional, for personalization)
- Curl type (optional, from quiz/guide download)
- Content preferences (inferred from clicks)
- Purchase history (from merch store)
- Engagement score (calculated from opens/clicks)

## Metrics & Reporting (for Sponsors)

### Email KPIs
| Metric | Target | Industry Avg |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Open Rate | 35%+ | 21% |
| Click Rate | 5%+ | 2.6% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.3% | 0.1% |
| Revenue per Email | Track | Varies |

### Sponsor-Specific Metrics
- Promo code redemptions
- Click-through to sponsor site
- Revenue attributed (via UTM tracking)
- Engagement by segment
- A/B test results on subject lines and CTAs

### Reporting Schedule
- **Bronze sponsors**: Monthly summary
- **Gold sponsors**: Bi-weekly report + quarterly strategy call
- **Platinum sponsors**: Weekly report + monthly strategy session

## Technical Implementation

### Recommended Tools
- **Email Platform**: ConvertKit or Beehiiv (creator-focused, automation-ready)
- **Analytics**: UTM parameters on all sponsor links
- **Tracking**: Unique promo codes per campaign
- **Integration**: Webhook from website forms → email platform

### UTM Convention
```
?utm_source=newsletter
&utm_medium=email
&utm_campaign={flow-name}
&utm_content={sponsor-name}
&utm_term={segment}
```

### Automation Triggers
- Form submission → Welcome flow
- Episode click → Listener nurture
- Merch waitlist → Merch flow
- 60-day inactivity → Re-engagement
- Tag-based → Sponsor spotlight

## Growth Tactics

### List Building
- **Homepage**: Newsletter popup (timed, exit-intent)
- **Episode pages**: "Get the show notes" email gate
- **Blog**: Content upgrade downloads (curl type guide, product checklist)
- **Merch**: Waitlist capture
- **Social**: Link-in-bio to landing page with freebie
- **Podcast**: Verbal CTA in every episode

### Referral Program
- "Share the Collective" — subscribers get unique referral link
- Rewards: early merch access, exclusive content, community badge
- Sponsors can co-fund rewards (e.g., free product for 5 referrals)

*This strategy positions email as the #1 conversion channel for both community growth and sponsor ROI. Every email delivers value first — sponsorships are woven in naturally, never feeling like ads.*
