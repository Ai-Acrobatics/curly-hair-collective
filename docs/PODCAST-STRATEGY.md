# Curlie Girlie Collective — Podcast Strategy & Year 1 Plan

## Who We Are

**Sabrina Anderson** (@fabsabstyle) and **Chanelle** (@createdbychanelle) — two women in our 20s and 30s who:
- **Came to the Lord later in life** — our faith journey is central to everything
- **Both work at Jon Renau** as alternative hair educators
- **Sabrina**: "JESUS, then everything else" — Educator @jonrenau, Curly Girl in San Diego, CA
- **Chanelle**: "God's Girl & Powered by Prayer" — On-Set Hairstylist, Licensed Cosmetologist, Alternative Hair Educator & Product Specialist @jonrenau
- **Specialize in** wigs, toppers, and hair replacement systems for people experiencing hair loss (chemo, autoimmune conditions, alopecia)
- **Want to build a ministry** through authentic conversation

## Core Mission

**"Anybody can come to the Lord no matter where you're at in your walk."**

We're building a podcast that sits at the intersection of:
1. **Faith** — Encouragement, testimony, growing in Christ
2. **Identity & Hair** — Alternative hair education, hair loss journeys, self-image
3. **Real Talk** — Two friends being honest about who we are now vs. who we're becoming

## Target Market Analysis

### Primary Audience

| Segment | Who They Are | Why They Listen |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Women experiencing hair loss** | Ages 25-55, going through chemo, alopecia, autoimmune conditions, postpartum loss, PCOS | Need practical help AND emotional/spiritual encouragement. Feel alone. |
| **Women exploring wigs/toppers** | Any age, curious about alternative hair. May be new to it or upgrading. | Want education from trusted sources, not just sales pitches. |
| **Women on a faith journey** | 20s-40s, came to faith later in life or re-exploring faith. May feel "too far gone." | Need to hear "you're not too late" from people who GET it. |
| **Christian women seeking real community** | 20s-40s, tired of performative faith, want raw honest conversation | Want authenticity, not religious performance. |

### Secondary Audience

| Segment | Who They Are | Why They Listen |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Hair professionals** | Stylists, salon owners, wig specialists | Learning alternative hair education to serve clients better |
| **Caregivers** | Husbands, mothers, friends of someone experiencing hair loss | Want to understand and support their loved one |
| **Women in personal growth** | Anyone who resonates with "becoming who I'm meant to be" | The transformation narrative is universally compelling |

### What They're Searching For

**Emotional needs:**
- "Am I still beautiful?" (after hair loss)
- "Is God really for me?" (late-in-life faith)
- "I feel like I'm starting over" (identity shift)
- Permission to be imperfect in their faith walk

**Practical needs:**
- Which wig/topper is right for me?
- How do I maintain alternative hair?
- How do I talk to my doctor about hair loss options?
- How do I start/restart a relationship with God?

## The Podcast Format

### Show Structure: "Just Talk" + Purpose

**Episode Length:** 35-50 minutes
**Release Schedule:** Weekly (every Tuesday — "Transform Tuesday")
**Format:** 70% co-host conversation, 30% guest interviews

### Episode Types (Rotating)

| Type | Description | Frequency |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Real Talk** | Just the two of you — life, faith, growth, honest conversation | 2x/month |
| **Hair Stories** | Guest shares their hair loss journey + how they found confidence | 2x/month |
| **Faith Journey** | Someone shares their testimony — came to God from unexpected places | 1x/month |
| **Wig 101** | Educational episode: product reviews, tips, tutorials (pairs with video) | 1x/month |
| **Q&A / Mailbag** | Listener questions about faith, hair, identity | 1x/month |

### The Ministry Model

This isn't just a business — it's a ministry that sustains itself. Here's how to think about it:

- **Free content** (podcast, social) = your outreach / evangelism
- **Digital products** = serve people who want to go deeper
- **Community** = discipleship and ongoing support
- **Events** = in-person connection and testimony
- **Revenue** = funds the ministry so you can keep going

**Tithe your podcast income.** This reinforces the ministry identity and keeps you grounded.
