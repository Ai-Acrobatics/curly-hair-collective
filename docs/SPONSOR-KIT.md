# Curlie Girlie Collective — Sponsor & Partnership Kit

## About Us

**Curlie Girlie Collective** is a podcast and digital community celebrating curly hair culture through conversations, education, and empowerment. We connect with a passionate, engaged audience that trusts our recommendations and actively seeks products that serve their natural hair needs.

Our audience doesn't just listen — they act. We've built trust through honest, expert-backed content that never compromises authenticity for a sponsorship.

### Niche but Growing
The natural hair care market is valued at $10B+ and growing. Our audience is at the center of this movement — educated, product-savvy, and ready to invest in quality.

### Multi-Platform Reach
We're not just a podcast. Our community spans audio, web, social media, email, and live events — giving sponsors multiple touchpoints with our audience.

## Ad Format Specifications

### Pre-Roll (15 seconds)
- Host-read, conversational tone
- Brand name, tagline, key value prop
- CTA (URL or promo code)
- Placed at episode opening

### Mid-Roll (60 seconds)
- Host-read with personal endorsement
- Product/service description
- Personal anecdote or experience (when applicable)
- CTA with promo code
- Placed at natural content break

### Post-Roll (30 seconds)
- Thank-you mention with CTA recap
- Available as add-on to any tier

### Native Content
- Full blog post (800-1200 words)
- Product review with honest assessment
- Integration into relevant episode topics
- Social media carousel or reel
